Pawell - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Pawell values your privacy and your pet's privacy. This privacy policy aims to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide, as well as your rights regarding this information.

Information Collected

Pet Information

You may choose to provide the following information to the app:

  • Pet's basic information (name, breed, age, gender, etc.)
  • Pet's health records (vaccinations, weight changes, health events, etc.)
  • Pet's photos

User Information

We may collect the following user information:

  • Device identifiers (for app functionality and error reporting)
  • App usage data (such as usage frequency, feature usage)

Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining app functionality
  • Storing and managing pet health data
  • Sending reminder notifications (such as vaccination reminders)
  • Generating health reports
  • Improving app performance and user experience

Information Storage

Your pet's health data is stored in the following locations:

  • Local storage on your device
  • Apple iCloud (if you choose to enable cloud sync functionality)

We do not store your pet's health data on our servers, nor do we share this data with third parties.

Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Using device local encrypted storage
  • Leveraging Apple iCloud's security features (if cloud sync is enabled)
  • Regularly updating the app to fix security vulnerabilities

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud (for data synchronization)
  • Apple Push Notification Service (for sending reminders)

The use of these third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and edit your pet's health data
  • Export your pet's health data
  • Delete your pet's health data
  • Disable cloud sync functionality

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published in the app or on our website, and your continued use of the app constitutes your agreement to the updated privacy policy.
